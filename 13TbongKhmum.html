Tboung Khmum is located on the central lowlands of the Mekong River. Its capital and largest city is Suong. The name Tboung Khmum means Amber, the translucent honey colour fossilized resin; Tboung for gem and khmum means bee.

The province shares an international border with Vietnam to the east.
